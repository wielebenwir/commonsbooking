# Documentation

This documentation applies to CommonsBooking > 2.0.0.

# Finding your way around

[Get started with the installation](setup/install).
Familiarize yourself with the [basics](basics/) to understand the most important terms and concepts.
In [First Steps](first-steps/) you will learn how to make your items bookable.
Once that is done, you can choose from many options to [display your booking offers in the frontend and customize fields](administration/).
Finally, you can [customize your email templates to perfectly fit CommonsBooking to your organization](administration/template-tags).
While operating, you can then [manage bookings](manage-bookings/) and [export them for analysis](settings/export). If you have problems, our [FAQ](faq/) might help. With the [API](api/) you can also connect external services.

### Any questions, need for support or found a bug?

[Submit a ticket using our support system (English and German)](https://support.commonsbooking.org).
You will also find an FAQ with frequently asked questions and answers there.
