# Finding your way around
[Get started with the installation](./setup/install).
Familiarize yourself with the [basics (not translated yet)](./basics/) to understand the most important terms and concepts.
In [First Steps](./first-steps/) you will learn how to make your items bookable.
Once that is done, you can choose from many options to [display your booking offers in the frontend and customize fields (not translated yet)](./administration/).
Finally, you can [customize your email templates to perfectly fit CommonsBooking to your organization (not translated yet)](./settings/).
While operating, you can then [manage bookings (not translated yet)](./manage-bookings/) and [export them for analysis](./settings/export). If you have problems, our [FAQ (not translated yet)](./faq/) might help. With the [API (not translated yet)](./api/) you can also connect external services.

### Any questions, need for support or found a bug?
